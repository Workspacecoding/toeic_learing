Day 7：交通觀光

📘 單字精選

  • destination (目的地) — 常見用法：travel destination
    例句：Paris is a popular travel destination.
  • itinerary (行程) — 常見用法：plan an itinerary
    例句：He planned the itinerary carefully.
  • transportation (交通工具) — 常見用法：public transportation
    例句：Public transportation is convenient.
  • luggage (行李) — 常見用法：pack luggage
    例句：She packed her luggage last night.
  • accommodation (住宿) — 常見用法：book accommodation
    例句：They booked their accommodation online.

🧠 文法觀念

助動詞 can/must — 表達能力或義務
