Day 5：行政文書

📘 單字精選

  • agenda (議程) — 常見用法：set the agenda
    例句：The manager set the agenda for the meeting.
  • circulate (傳閱) — 常見用法：circulate a memo
    例句：Please circulate the memo to all departments.
  • notify (通知) — 常見用法：notify the team
    例句：HR notified the team about the changes.
  • attachment (附件) — 常見用法：email attachment
    例句：Check the attachment in the email.
  • deadline (截止期限) — 常見用法：meet the deadline
    例句：We must meet the deadline this week.
